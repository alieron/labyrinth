### Concept
#### Frequency aliasing
- two distinct continuous [signals](/labyrinth/notes/cs/cs2108/time-domain_signals) produce the same sequence of discrete sample values
- when sampled at a fixed sampling frequency

$$
\text{Alias of }f:\quad f' = f + k \cdot f_{s}, \quad k \in \mathbb{Z}
$$
