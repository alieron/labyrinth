### Summary
Even functions ^938d30
- function that is symmetric about the y-axis

$$
f(x) = f(-x)
$$

Odd function
- function that is antisymmetric about the y-axis

$$
\begin{align*}
f(x) &= -f(-x) \\
\\
f(-x) &= -f(x)
\end{align*}
$$
