### Summary
Polar coordinate system
![[polar_coords.png]]

Converting between cartesian and polar
$$
\begin{gather*}
x = r\cos\theta\\
y = r\sin\theta\\
\\
r=\sqrt{ x^{2}+y^{2} }\\
\tan\theta=\frac{y}{x}
\end{gather*}
$$

Polar rectangle
$$
R = \{\begin{array}{c|c} (r,\theta) & a\leq r\leq b \land \alpha \leq \theta \leq\beta \end{array}\}
$$
