Euler's formula ^df8f33
$$
\begin{gather*}
e^{i\theta} = \cos \theta + i\sin\theta \\
e^{-i\theta} = \cos \theta - i\sin\theta 
\end{gather*}
$$

$$
|e^{i\theta}| = \sqrt{ \cos ^{2}\theta + \sin ^{2}\theta } = 1
$$

Sine and cosine as complext exponentials ^83fa5e
$$
\begin{align*}
\cos x &= \frac{e^{ix}-e^{-ix}}{2i} \\
\\
\sin x &= \frac{e^{ix}-e^{-ix}}{2}
\end{align*}
$$
