Group axioms
$$
\begin{align*}
&&& (G,*) \text{ satisfies} \\
\\
\text{(G1)} &&& \forall a,b\in G,\ a*b\in G &&\text{(Closure)} \\
\\
\text{(G2)} &&& \forall a,b,c \in G, \ (a*b)*c=a*(b*c) && \text{(Associativity)}\\
\\
\text{(G3)} &&& \exists e\in G,\ \forall a\in G,\ e*a=a*e=a && \text{(Identity)}\\
\\
\text{(G4)} &&& \forall a\in G,\ \exists b\in G,\ a*b=b*a=e && \text{(Inverse)}
\end{align*}
$$

Uniqueness of the identity
$$
\begin{gather*}
\text{let }e, e'\text{ be identity elements of }(G,*) \\
\\
e=e*e'=e'& \text{(G3)}\\
\therefore e=e' \\
&&\square
\end{gather*}
$$
