### Concept
#### Hilbert space
- extension of the [inner product space](/labyrinth/notes/math/abstract_algebra/inner_product#^40f0f2) to infinite terms/dimensions

$$
\begin{align*}
&&&H\text{ is a Hilbert space} \iff  \\
\\
1) &&& H\text{ is an inner product space} && \text{(Inner Product Space)} \\
2) &&& H\text{ is Cauchy complete} && \text{(Cauchy Complete)}
\end{align*}
$$

Inner product of hilbert space
$$
\braket{ f | g } =\int_{a}^{b} f(t)g(t)^* \ dt
$$

L space
$$
L^{2}[a,b]=\left\{\begin{array}{c|c} f & \int_{a}^{b}|f(x)|^{2} \ dx < \infty\end{array}\right\} \\
$$
