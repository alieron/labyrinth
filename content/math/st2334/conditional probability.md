### Summary
Law of total probability
$$
P(B)=\sum_{i=1}^{n} P(B\cap A_{i}) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} P(A_{i})P(B|A_{i})
$$

Special case of 2 events
$$
P(B)=P(A)P(B|A)+P(A')P(B|A')
$$
