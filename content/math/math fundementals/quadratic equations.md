### Summary
$$
\begin{align*}
f(x) & = ax^2 + bx + c &&\text{(Normal form)}\\
& = a(x-p)(x-q) &&\text{(Root form)}\\
& = a(x-h)^2+k &&\text{(Completing the square)}\\
\\
\text{Vertex :} \qquad (h, \ k) & = \left(\frac{-b}{2a}, \ \frac{4ac-b^2}{4a}\right)\\
\\
\text{Line of symmetry :} \qquad x & = \frac{-b}{2a}
\end{align*}
$$
