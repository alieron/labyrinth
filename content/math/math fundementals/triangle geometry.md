### Summary
$$
\begin{align*}
\text{Sine rule :}& \qquad \frac{a}{\sin A}=\frac{b}{\sin B}=\frac{c}{\sin C} \\
\\
\text{Cosine rule :}& \qquad c^2=a^2+b^2-2ab  \cos C \\
\\
\text{Area of triangle :}& \qquad \frac{1}{2}ab \sin C
\end{align*}
$$
